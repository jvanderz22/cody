ABOUT ME

I am a lead programmer and effects artist at Dark Moth Studios in Grand Rapids, MI, and I am completing a bachelor’s degree in digital animation and game design from Ferris State University, one of the Princeton Review’s top 25 programs for game design.

In 2013, I studied abroad in the Netherlands, collaborating with other international students on Sonic Sprint Submarine, an educational game for the Heim Technical Museum in Hengelo, Netherlands.

Now, I am working with Dark Moth Studios on a horror game called Linger, I am programming the game in Unreal Engine 4, as well as creating special effects.

When I have spare time, you can find me with a controller in my hands, or with a soccer ball at my feet.

Please feel free to contact me by Facebook or email.
